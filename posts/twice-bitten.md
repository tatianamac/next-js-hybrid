---
title: 'Twice Bitten'
date: '2020-02-01'
---

Smells like soft musk
Arms quarter past
Legs doubled stairs
The scent of rush

Tastes like being heard
My words, your lips
In well-timed lulls
Conversational eclipse

Feels like books
Pages, sheets upturned
Weathered, bent spines
Heated by words burnt
